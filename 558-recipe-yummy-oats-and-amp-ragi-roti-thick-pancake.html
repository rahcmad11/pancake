Ingredients of Oats & ragi roti/thick pancake

  1. You need 1 cup of ragi flour.
  2. It's 1 cup of oats(i used steel cut oats powdered in coffee grinder).
  3. You need 1/2 cup of roasted peanuts crushed.
  4. It's 1 of small onion chopped.
  5. Prepare 2 of green chilli.
  6. Prepare 1 handful of Cilantro chopped.
  7. It's 1 tsp of cumin seeds & salt to taste.

Oats & ragi roti/thick pancake step by step

  1. Add all the ingredients in a wide bowl.
  2. Mix it well with water to make a thick batter consistency.
  3. Spread the batter into the medium hot pan gently & pour a tsp of oil around the roti & let it cook for 2 mins.
  4. Flip over to cook the other side & let it cook for 2 more mins..
  5. Spread some ghee on top & serve hot with chutney. Enjoy!!.
