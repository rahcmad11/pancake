Pancake with condensed milk step by step

  1. Mix condensed milk and cooking oil until well combined..
  2. Add flour, baking powder and baking soda..
  3. Mix until fully incorporated with no lumps..
  4. Heat pan first with meduim fire. If it is already hot enough lower fire to medium low heat..
  5. You can use any size of spoon or cups you want, however big or small you want you pancake to be. I use 1/2 cup per pancake..
