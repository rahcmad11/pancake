Ingredients of Eggplant pancakes

  1. It's 1 of eggplant.
  2. It's 1/4 cup of oil (for pan frying).
  3. You need 1 of egg.
  4. You need 2-3 Tbsp of flour.
  5. Prepare 1-2 Tbsp of butter (softened).
  6. It's 1-2 Tbsp of brown sugar.
  7. Prepare 1-2 tsp of baking powder.
  8. Prepare of Powdered Sugar (if preferred, my daughter likes maple syrup).

Preheat a hot griddle or large skillet (cast iron is best). Brush with oil, butter, lard or shortening. Trim off the end and cut the eggplant in half lengthwise. Place the two halves cut-side down on a baking sheet.

Eggplant pancakes step by step

  1. First, everything is dependent on the size of your eggplant, and how many pancakes you want. 1 cup of chopped, uncooked eggplant makes 4 (3in) pancakes. Use the smaller amount of ingredients for 4-6 pancakes, and the larger amount to double. Do Not add an extra egg, though:-).
  2. Steam the chopped eggplant until cooked, about 10min. Let cool on a towel lined plate..
  3. Heat oil to a medium (5) in a large pan, skillet, with deep sides preferred..
  4. In a mixing bowl, beat egg w/ softened butter. Add flour, baking powder, brown sugar, and eggplant. Mash the eggplant and combine..
  5. Place by 'spoonful' (I pour right from mixing bowl) into hot oil. Cook until bubbly on top, then flip. Remove when nice and browned, place on a towel lined plate. (they will look like pancakes).
