Mini Corn Pancakes instructions

  1. Take besan in a bowl and add all the other ingredients except oil. Add water and Mix well to a smooth batter without lumps. The batter should not be to thin or too thick. Check the seasonings and keep aside to rest for ten minutes..
  2. Heat a pan and brush with some oil or butter. Pour a ladle of batter and let it cook from both sides until golden brown and crisp.
  3. Serve hot with salsa and mayonnaise or sauce or chutney..
