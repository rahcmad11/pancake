Mom's Fluffy Pancakes instructions

  1. Combine milk with vinegar in a medium bowl and set aside for 5 minutes to "sour".
  2. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt into a large mixing bowl..
  3. Whisk egg and butter into "soured" milk..
  4. Pour flour mixture into wet ingredients and whisk until lumps are gone..
  5. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and coat with cooking spray..
  6. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto skillet and cook until bubbles appear on the surface. Flip with a spatula and cook until browned on the other side..
