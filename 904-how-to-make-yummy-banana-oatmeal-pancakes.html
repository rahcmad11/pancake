Ingredients of Banana Oatmeal Pancakes

  1. You need 1 1/2 cup of organic milk.
  2. It's 1 cup of rolled oats.
  3. It's 2 of bananas, chopped.
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of all purpose flour.
  5. Prepare 1 1/4 tablespoon of baking powder.
  6. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of salt.

Banana Oatmeal Pancakes instructions

  1. Pour milk and oats in a blender and blend until smooth. Add bananas, flour, salt and baking powder. Puree a few seconds more. Let batter rest 10 minutes..
  2. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add a few drops of avocado oil and scoop 1/4 cup batter into pan. Reduce heat to medium low and cook pancakes until air bubbles appear and underside is golden brown (about 5 minutes). Flip and cook 5 minutes more..
  3. Top with your favourite sliced fruit, berries and maple syrup if desired..
