Ingredients of Vegan Mounds Pancakes, first way

  1. You need 1 cup of old fashioned baking mix, such as Bisquick.
  2. You need 1 cup of sweetened vanilla coconut milk.
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp of coconut oil.
  4. Prepare 1 of flax egg (1:1 ratio of ground flax:water, set).
  5. Prepare 1 of \4 cup vegan chocolate chips.
  6. You need 1 of \2 cup shredded coconut (I used organic, dehydrated).
  7. Prepare of chocolate syrup (optional).

Vegan Mounds Pancakes, first way step by step

  1. Combine all ingredients in medium mixing bowl..
  2. Drop 1\4 cup sized dollops onto hot/preheated pan (my medium pan on large burner was set to 3, so medium heat)..
  3. Let cook each side approx. 3-5 minutes until center stops bubbling and edges lift with ease..
  4. Serve with chocolate syrup and shredded coconut (not shown), or just devour them!.
