Ingredients of Oats Semolina pancakes

  1. Prepare 2 cup of semolina (sooji) roasted.
  2. You need 1 cup of oats roasted.
  3. You need 1 cup of mix veg(Capsicum, onion, tomato, green chilli) chopped.
  4. Prepare 5 tsp of Oil.
  5. It's to taste of Salt.
  6. It's 1Tsp of Red chilli powder.
  7. It's 2 cups of Water.
  8. Prepare 1 cup of curd.

Oats Semolina pancakes instructions

  1. Finely grind the roasted semolina and oats and take them in a bowl.
  2. Add salt to taste, curd and water and mix well leaving no lumps and let rest for 20 minutes.
  3. Add water if the batter has become thick, the batter should not be too thick, neither slurry.
  4. Add the veggies to the batter.
