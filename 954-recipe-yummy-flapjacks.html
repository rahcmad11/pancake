Ingredients of Flapjacks

  4. It's 170 g of rolled oats.

Flapjacks instructions

  1. Heat butter, sugar and golden syrup.
  2. Remove from heat and add oats.
  3. Press into a 20cm greased tin and cook for 10mins at 175oC. Leave to cool for 5min then remove from tin..
