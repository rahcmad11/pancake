Ingredients of Gluten Free Dairy Free Banana Chocolate Chip Pancakes

  1. You need 1 of Banana.
  2. Prepare 2 of Eggs.
  3. You need 1/4 cup of Quick oats.
  4. Prepare 1 tsp of Honey.
  5. You need 1/3 cup of Chocolate chips/unsweetened carob chips.

Gluten Free Dairy Free Banana Chocolate Chip Pancakes step by step

  1. Combine all ingredients except the chocolate chips in the blender and blend on high until everything is fully integrated.
  2. Then ad the chocolate chips to your batter then pour on a pan and cook your pancakes on medium heat.
  3. Flip after a couple minutes or when the edges of the pancakes get bubbly.
  4. Cook until they are just how you like them. Ad fruit on top if you like, and then enjoy!.
