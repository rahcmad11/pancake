Spinach pancakes step by step

  1. Seperate 2 eggs. Whip the 2 egg whites until they get hard..
  2. In an other bowl mix the 2 egg yolks and 1 whole egg with the flour(I used cornflour but you can use any other type of oil). Then add the spinach and mix it very well..
  3. Add the whipped egg whites to the egg yolk&spinach and mix them gently. Season it as you like. I added salt, pepper and some garlic..
  4. Take 1 tablespoonful of the pastry and cook it on the frying pan. It's like you're making a pancake..
  5. For me this recipe made 11 pancakes..
