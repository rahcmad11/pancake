Jaalar Appam || Net pancakes || Malaysian net crepes || Roti Jaala step by step

  1. Add Onion and fennel seeds in a mixer grind it along with little amount of water and strain the onion juice..
  2. In another mixer add all purpose flour, onion juice, coconut milk, water, egg, salt, turmeric powder.Grind it to a smooth batter without lumps.
  3. Now transfer the batter into the crepe dispenser with 5nozzles,start pour or design according to your wish in a pan and apply some ghee on it..
