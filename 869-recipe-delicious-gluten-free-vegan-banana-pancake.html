Ingredients of Gluten free vegan banana pancake

  1. It's 270 grams of gluten free flour.
  2. You need 2 tsp of baking powder.
  3. It's 1/2 tsp of salt.
  4. You need 1 tsp of cinnamon (optional).
  5. You need 230 ml of dairy free milk (almond).
  6. It's 2 tbsp of maple syrup.
  7. You need 2 tsp of vanilla extract.
  8. It's 1 tbsp of oil (coconut).
  9. You need 2 of ripe bananas.
