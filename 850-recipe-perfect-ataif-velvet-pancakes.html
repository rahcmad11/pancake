  7. You need of vegetableoil, for brushing.

Ataif Velvet Pancakes step by step

  1. In a small bowl or cup,dissolve yeast & sugar in milk & leave for 5minutes,or until foamy. In a large bowl, mix flour &baking powder. Stir in yeast mitxure.Gradually stir in water until combined Cover with a kitchen towel & let in a warm place for 45minutes,or until foamy..
  2. Heat aheavy_bottomed skillet over medium heat. Brush with vegetable oil. Using a small ladle, spoon 1tables batter onto the skillet for eash pancakes. Cook pancakes on one side only for about 3minutes,or until bubbly & dry on tops &lights golden on the bottoms.Adjust heat as necessary to cook pancakes evenly. Repeat to cook remaining pancakes..
  3. Place a pancakes,uncooked side up, on a work surface. Fold pancake in half &pinch 2/3 of the outer edge closed, forming acone..
  4. Fill each cone with agenerous tablespoon of Clotted Cream arrange on a serving plate.Granish with pistachio..
  5. Serve Atife with sugar syrup to drizzle over the top as desired..
