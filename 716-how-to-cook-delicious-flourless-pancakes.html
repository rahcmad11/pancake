Flourless Pancakes step by step

  1. mash the banana..
  2. beat the eggs..
  3. mix mashed banana and beaten eggs (you may add optional items of your choice. I like flaxseed or cinnamon powder). make sure you don't add too much banana into the mixture as the pancake may fall apart..
  4. heat skillet and add little butter. small-medium heat will do..
  5. pour the mixture onto the skillet. I find it easier to toss smaller pancakes..
