Ingredients of Banana Oat Pancakes

  1. You need 1 1/4 Cup of Oats.
  2. Prepare 4 of Bananas for the Dough (1/2 Banana for plating).
  3. You need 3 of Eggs.
  4. Prepare 1/4 Teaspoon of Cinnamon.
  5. It's 1 Tablespoon of Vanilla.
  6. You need 1 1/2 Tablespoon of Baking soda.
  7. It's Pinch of Salt.
  8. Prepare of Blueberries (to your preference).
  9. It's 1 Teaspoon of Peanut Butter (for each pancake layer).
  10. Prepare 1 Teaspoon of Honey.

Banana Oat Pancakes step by step

  1. Mash the bananas and add cinnamon.
  2. Add eggs to the mashed bananas.
  3. Add vanilla flavor.
  4. Baking soda and a pinch of salt.
  5. Add 1 cup and 1/4 oats.
  6. Blend the mix in a blender.
  7. Add blue berries before frying the dough with coconut oil.
  8. Add peanut butter and banana after frying and sprinkle cinnamon and a teaspoon of honey on top..
