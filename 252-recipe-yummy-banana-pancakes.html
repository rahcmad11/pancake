Banana Pancakes instructions

  1. Take banana and eggs. Mash the bananas well with your hand or fork..
  2. Beat the eggs well along with bananas using a whisk to a smooth mixture. Keep it ready..
  3. Heat a tawa or iron skillet, add a small laddle of pancake mix to it and spread it to form a thin circles..
  4. Don't spread too much. Sprinkle oil or butter. Cook until brown on one side for five minutes and flip the pancakes carefully..
  5. Cook for a minute and remove from tawa. Repeat the same process for the remaining mix..
  6. Garnish with kiwi slices,chocolate gems..
