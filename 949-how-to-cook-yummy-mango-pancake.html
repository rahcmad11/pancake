Ingredients of Mango Pancake

  1. Prepare 1 of mango puree.
  2. You need 1 cup of maida/All purpose flour.
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp of sugar.
  4. Prepare 1 tsp of baking powder.
  5. You need 1/4 tsp of cinnamon powder.
  6. You need to taste of Salt.
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of milk.

Mango Pancake step by step

  2. Add 1tsp baking powder mix it well.Batter is ready.Heat a non-stick pan put a laddleful batter..
  3. Cover it a lid.Cook 2 mints,than flip it &cook another 2 mints. Cook on low flame.Pan cake is ready..
  4. Garnishing with Honey& cinnamonpowder serve it..
