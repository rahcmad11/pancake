Ingredients of Apple Banana Oatmeal Pancakes

  1. Prepare 1 of apple (diced).
  2. Prepare 2 of very ripe bananas.
  3. You need 1/2-1 cup of oatmeal.
  4. Prepare 1/2-1 cup of bisquick mix.
  5. You need 2-3 of eggs.
  6. You need 1 tbs of vanilla extract.
  7. You need 1 tbs of cinnamon.
  8. It's 2 tbs of blackberry honey.
