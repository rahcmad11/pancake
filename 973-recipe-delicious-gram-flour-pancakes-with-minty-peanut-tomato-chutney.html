Gram Flour Pancakes with Minty Peanut Tomato Chutney instructions

  1. In a big bowl take all the ingredients of gram flour pancakes,mix it nicely and make a smooth and thick batter..
  2. Heat a pan,pour 2 tsp oil and spread it nicely,pour a laddle full of batter and gently spread it with the spatula and cook it for 3-4 mi utes on low flame..
  3. Flip it and pour 1 tsp more oil and shallow fry the pancake from another side also until it becomes golden and crispy..
  4. Make all the pancakes with the same process..
  5. In a mixer jar add all the ingredients of chutney and grind it until it becomes a smooth paste..
  6. Chutney is ready..
  7. Gram Flour Pancakes is ready to serve with Minty Peanut Tomato Chutney..
