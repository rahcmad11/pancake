Ingredients of Cardamom Raisin Flapjacks

  1. It's 145 g of dairy free spread.
  2. Prepare 50 g of brown sugar.
  3. You need 3 tablespoons of honey.
  4. Prepare 235 g of gluten free porridge oats.
  5. It's 1 teaspoon of cinnamon.
  6. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of cardamom.
  7. You need 80 g of raisins.
