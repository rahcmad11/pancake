Ingredients of Blueberry pancakes with blueberry molasses syrup

  1. It's 2 cup of fresh blueberries.
  2. It's 1 1/2 cup of self rising flour.
  3. It's 2 of eggs.
  4. You need 3 tbsp of melted butter.
  5. You need 1 cup of milk.
  6. You need 2 tbsp of honey.
  7. It's 1 tsp of vanilla extract.
  8. Prepare 1/2 tbsp of cinnamon.
  9. It's 1/2 cup of molasses.

Blueberry pancakes with blueberry molasses syrup step by step

  1. Combine dry ingredients in a medium bowl and mix well.
  2. form a well in the middle of the dry ingredients by digging a hole in the middle.
  3. pour milk, eggs, honey, and vanilla in the well and mix well.
  4. fold 1 cup of blueberries into pancake batter.
  5. scoop pancake batter onto hot, buttered griddle. I keep my griddle between 3-4 out of 10 and melt butter on it in the beginning. I scoop the batter out using 1/3 cup scoop..
  6. Flip pancakes once bubbles form in the batter..
  7. Keep pancakes warm in 170° oven on a cooling rack..
  8. Sometime during pancake making, place 1 cup blueberries and molasses in a saute pan and cover. Slowly bring to a boil and boil for 5 min. Remove from heat.
  9. Pile pancakes on a plate and cover with syrup!.
