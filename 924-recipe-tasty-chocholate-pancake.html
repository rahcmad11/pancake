Chocholate pancake step by step

  1. For making chocholate pancakes in a bowl add 1 and 1/4cup of Maida, to that add 1/4 cocoa powder and 1/4 cup of powdered sugar..
  2. Now take 1 and 1/4 cup of milk, to that add 2 tbsp of butter / ghee. Mix it until butter is fully melted. Now gradually that milk into that batter and make a batter of flowing consistancy..
  3. Now add 1/2 tsp of baking powder and 1 /4 tsp of baking soda and mix it well.
  4. Take a non stick Tawa and heat on low flame for few minutes. Now using a serving spoon take 1 and 1/2 serving spoon of batter and pour it to the Tawa and cook it on low flame for couple of minutes..
  5. Now when the surface of pancake starts bubble up try remove the pancake very gently, and if it is removed from the surface of Tawa wait for few seconds until the top is little bit dry and then flip it. The moment you flip it the pancake will rise and then finally take it out of flame..
  6. Now make all pancakes like this. Now take two pancakes apply nuttela or any other choco spread between them and squeeze some Chocholate syrup on top these steps are optional but it really make the pancake like a Dora cake..
