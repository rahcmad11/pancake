Ingredients of Apricot and pecan flapjack

  1. You need 200 g of oats.
  2. Prepare 100 g of butter or margarine.
  3. Prepare 50 g of brown sugar.
  4. You need 4 tbsp of honey or dandelion honey.
  5. You need 50 g of dried apricots.
  6. It's 50 g of whole pecans.

Apricot and pecan flapjack instructions

  1. Melt butter, sugar and honey in a pan or microwave..
  2. Stir in oats until well coated with mixture..
  3. Add roughly chopped apricots and pecans and stir to combine..
  4. Pour into a lined baking tin and level by pressing with the back of a spoon. Pit into a preheated oven at 180C for 15 minutes until lightly golden and beginning to pull away from the sides..
  5. Cool completely before cutting into small pieces. Enjoy warm with custard as a yummy desert or cold as a delicious snack..
