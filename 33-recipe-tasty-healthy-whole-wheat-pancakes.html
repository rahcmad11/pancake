Recipe: Tasty Healthy whole wheat pancakes

Delicious, fresh and tasty.


Healthy whole wheat pancakes.

You can cook Healthy whole wheat pancakes using 11 ingredients and 7 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Healthy whole wheat pancakes

  1. It's 1 cup of whole wheat flour.
  2. You need 1 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder.
  3. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda.
  4. You need 1/4 teaspoon of salt.
  5. You need 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon.
  6. Prepare 1 cup of milk.
  7. You need 1 tablespoon of white vinegar.
  8. You need 1 of egg.
  9. Prepare 2 tablespoons of coconut oil.
  10. It's 2 tablespoons of honey.
  11. It's 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract.

Healthy whole wheat pancakes step by step

  1. Mix 1 tablespoon of white vinegar with the milk, and set aside to sour for 5-10 minutes..
  2. Sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon..
  3. Beat one egg into the soured milk, and mix in the coconut oil, honey, and vanilla extract..
  4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients, and mix until all the lumps are gone..
  5. Heat a lightly greased skillet to medium heat. Scoop 1/4 cup of the batter onto the skillet..
  6. Cook until bubbles start to form at the top, then flip over to cook the other side..
  7. Serve with sliced honey, sliced bananas, and top with granola..
