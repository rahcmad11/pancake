Ingredients of Mango custard pancakes

  1. You need 1 1/2 cup of madia.
  2. You need 3 tbsp of custard powder.
  3. You need 1 bowl of mango pulp.
  4. It's 1/4 teaspoon of baking powder and soda.
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup of sugar(depending on the sweetness of the mango also).
  6. You need Few of drops of vanilla essence.
  7. You need 2 tbsp of butter.
  8. You need as required of Butter to fry.
  9. You need as required of Honey.
  10. It's 1 cup of milk.

Mango custard pancakes instructions

  1. First take the butter and sugar and whisk it till the sugar melts. Now in a bowl seive the madia and custard powder. Add baking powder and soda. Mix it well. Now add the butter mixture to it. Mix it. Now add vanilla essence and the mango pulp. Mix it well.
  2. Now add milk little by little so that the batter becomes smooth. It should not be too thick or thin. Now in a pan put butter. Spread the batter in round shape gently..
  3. Now cook it in flow flame by changing side. Serve it with honey and garnish it with diced mango..
