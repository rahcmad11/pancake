German Oven Pancake (AKA Hootenanny) step by step

  1. Blend flour, milk, eggs and salt..
  2. Pour batter into greased 9X13 pan..
  3. Pour melted butter on top of batter. Do not mix..
  4. Bake for 25 minutes at 400°F. Cut into squares and pour maple syrup over and serve..
