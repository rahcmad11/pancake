Ingredients of Broccoli & Parmesan Fritters (Mini Broccoli Pancakes)

  1. You need 4 cup of Steamed Broccoli.
  2. You need 1/2 cup of Flour.
  3. It's 1/3 cup of Grated Parmesan Cheese.
  4. It's 1 clove of Garlic, minced.
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup of Milk.
  6. You need 2 large of Eggs.
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp of Salt.
  8. You need 1 tbsp of Pepper.

Broccoli & Parmesan Fritters (Mini Broccoli Pancakes) instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl til batter has pancake like consistency..
  2. In a medium sized frying pan, add 3 tablespoons of vegetable oil and turn the burner to medium high heat..
  3. Drop individual rounded off tablespoons of your broccoli pancake batter into the hot oil. Fry each side til golden brown. That normally takes 45 seconds to 3 minutes depending on the heat of your burners..
  4. When finished frying to a golden brown, place on paper towels to drain. To garnish, sprinkle with a little parmesan..
