Ingredients of Mango Pancake

  1. It's 1/2 cup of - Mango.
  2. You need 1/2 cup of - Refined Flour.
  3. It's 1 tsp of - Ghee.
  4. You need 1 cup of - Milk.
  5. It's 4 tbsp of - Sugar.
  6. It's Pinch of yellow colour.

Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl, and add a few spoonfulls at a time to make the pancakes. Mango Pancake instructions

  1. Take mango in a mixture jar and make a puree..
  2. In a mixing bowl add,refined flour, ghee and sugar. Add mango puree. Mix it well..
  3. Now add milk little by little and mix it well, make a smooth thick batter..
  4. Add food colour, and Keep aside for 1hr..
  5. Heat a pan, and pour the batter. Cook both side till golden brown..
  6. Pancakes are ready to serve..
