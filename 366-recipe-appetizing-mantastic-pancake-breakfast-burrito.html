Ingredients of Mantastic Pancake Breakfast Burrito

  8. Prepare 5 of Eggs for Filling.

  10. It's 1 Cup of Mexican Cheese for Filling.

Mantastic Pancake Breakfast Burrito step by step

  1. In a large bowl mix all the ingredients for the Pancake Batter together..
  2. Fry bacon to your liking..
  3. Fry your scrambled eggs with your choice of oil or butter. I prefer using 5 tbsp of butter for a really heart clogging buttery taste..
  4. On a preheated griddle set on medium use 1/3 cup of batter per pancake..
  5. Cut bacon up and mix with eggs. Hit it with a dash of Black Pepper..
  6. Wrap the Egg, Bacon and Cheese inside the Pancake and Enjoy!.
