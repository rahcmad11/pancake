Coconut pancakes step by step

  1. In a bowl mix all dry ingredients together..
  2. Add all the wet ingredients to the beaten egg and whisk until well combined..
  3. Make a well in the dry ingredients and pour in wet ingredients and stir in well(do not over mix)..
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and grease lightly with little butter using a brush..
  5. Add 1/4 of the batter and spread to a rounded shape. Cook each side till golden brown. Repeat process till done..
  6. For the syrup: pour ingredients in a pan and leave to a boil..
  7. Take it off and leave it to cool..
