Ingredients of Banana egg oat pancake

  4. You need 1 tsp of vanilla extract.
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp of cinnamon.

Banana egg oat pancake step by step

  1. Mash banana, scramble eggs into banana mixture, add oats, vanilla and cinnamon, mix well..
